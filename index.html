IRB Submission Workflow

Follow each step in order

1
IRB Training
Have you completed IRB training? If not, complete it first using the link below, then return to this workflow.
🔗 IRB Training
2
Do you have an Approved Proposal Document?
Book or Dissertation — you need an approved Research Proposal
Business Plan — you need an approved Topic Proposal
3
Add Ethical Adherence Protocol to Your Research Proposal or Topic Proposal
Download the Ethical Adherence Protocol template and add it to your approved Research Proposal or Topic Proposal. This section must be embedded in the document — it is not a separate file.
🔗 Ethical Protocol
Save the updated document. This is the version you will submit to IRB.
4
Fill the IRB Application Form
Nothing may be left blank. Study start date must be at least 3 weeks from the IRB submission date. Data validity must not exceed 12 months from the data collection end date.
Open the IRB application and fill every field completely.
🔗 IRB Application
Set your Study Start Date — must be at least 3 weeks from the date you submit to IRB for approval.
Set your Study End Date and Data Validity End Date — data validity must not exceed 12 months from data collection end.
Take the signature of your chair on the completed application before submitting.
5
Vulnerable Population Check
Does your study involve a vulnerable population — minors, prisoners, cognitively impaired individuals, or others at risk of coercion?

Does your study involve specific vulnerable population — minors or the cognitively impaired individuals?

🛑 Stop — Change Your Research Subject

You must change your study population before proceeding. Revise your Research Proposal or Topic Proposal, obtain new chair approval, and restart from Step 2.

6
Institutional Consent
If you wish to conduct your study within an institution or organisation, you must obtain institutional consent before beginning data collection.
Download the institutional consent template, complete it, and get it signed by the relevant authority at the institution.
🔗 Institutional Consent
7
Plan Your Data Collection Strategy
Select all methods you will use. Each method requires a consent form and a questions / protocol document.
Consent must be Question 1 in the survey instrument. Also submit it as a standalone consent form.
Download and complete the survey informed consent form.
🔗 Consent Form
Prepare your survey questions with consent as Question 1.
🔗 Survey Questions
Download and complete the interview informed consent form.
🔗 Consent Form
Prepare your interview questions / protocol.
🔗 Interview Questions
Download and complete the focus group informed consent form.
🔗 Consent Form
Prepare your focus group discussion guide.
🔗 Discussion Guide
Download and complete the live study informed consent form.
🔗 Consent Form
Prepare your observation / study protocol.
🔗 Study Protocol
8
Zip All Files & Submit
Collect all your documents and zip them into a single file named exactly as shown below.
